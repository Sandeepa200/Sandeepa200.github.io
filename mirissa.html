Mirissa Beach

Mirissa is one of the most popular beach destinations in southern Sri Lanka. This beach facilitated with waves for surfing, spends time with the fresh coconut trees, eating delicious foods within the beach side.

In mirissa you can travel to parrot rock which is situated in front of the mirissa beach. This is one of the best thing to do in Mirissa tour. It can be dangerous to cross over during high tide. It is very popular to visit the sunset in the evening.

Coconut Tree Hill is one of the most popular spot in Sri Lanka and one of the top things to do in your Mirissa tour. The private coconut farm is a 15-minute walk from Mirissa Beach.
