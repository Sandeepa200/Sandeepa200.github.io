Colombo City

Colombo is the commercial capital city in Sri Lanka. This has the largest number of population in Sri Lanka as it is about 5.6 million. It is the financial center of the island and a best tourist destination.

There are many places to visit in Colombo. Some of them are Dutch Hospital which is dates back to the early 1600s, Galle Face Green is an open space faced to the beach, National Museum offers the Sri Lankan history, Viharamahadevi Park, Nelum kuluna etc. If you want to spend luxury vacation come to Colombo
