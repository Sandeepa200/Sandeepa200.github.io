Ambuluwawa tower

Ambuluwawa is a biodiversity complex situated in Gampola, Kandy Sri Lanka. This is the first multi-religious sanctuary situated in the central highlands of the country. It is located about 5 km from Gampola’s town center.

Top of the Ambuluwawa can experience an amazing view all over the area and a 360 degree views of the surrounding mountains, forests, rivers and towns. It may be scary and freak out climbers, especially those who have a fear of heights. Ambuluwawa is not at all dangerous or risky place to hike.

There are about 200 different kinds of plants from 80 plant families including a variety of medicinal plants. After reaching the top of the tower, mountains like Piduruthalagala from the East, Bathalegala from the West, Adam’s Peak from the South and Knuckles Mountain Range from the North can be get into your eyes.
