Sri Lanka

"The Pearl of the Indian Ocean"

Sri Lanka then known as Ceylon, and officially the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, is an island nation in South Asia. It is found in the Indian Ocean, southwest of the Bay of Bengal, and southeast of the Arabian Sea; separated from the Indian subcontinent by the Gulf of Mannar and thealk Strait. Sri Lanka shares a maritime border with India and the Maldives. Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte is its legal capital, and Colombo is a major city and financial center.
