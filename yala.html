Yala National Park Sri Lanka

Yala National park is a place for a crazy wildlife adventure in Sri Lanka and this is the most visited national park in Sri Lanka which covers an area of 979 square kilometers. Peacock on tree tops, walking along the road and bushes, Spotted deer and larger Sambur deer, giant elephants with a couple of cute little jumbos wandering in open grasslands are a common sight in Yala national park and Yala national park is the park with highest concentration of leopards in Sri Lanka.

This is 256 Km away from Colombo and 138 Km away from Kandy, The best way to reach the national park is by a car or a van.
